The Neuroscience

Psilocybin works primarily through 5-HT2A serotonin receptor agonism, temporarily disrupting the Default Mode Network (DMN) — the brain's autopilot that maintains your sense of self and habitual thought patterns. This creates a window of enhanced neuroplasticity where new neural connections form more easily.

Integration is the process of making those connections permanent. That's what this platform is built for.

FAQ

Questions answered

Is this legal?

100%. We don't sell any substances. We sell integration tools, educational content, breathwork, and a retreat directory. Education about psychedelics is protected speech. Harm reduction is encouraged by organizations like MAPS.

Is my journal data private?

Yes. End-to-end encryption. We never read your entries. We never sell your data. You can export or delete everything at any time.

Do I need to have used psychedelics to use this?

Not at all. Many of our users start with breathwork, meditation, or are simply curious. The journal and mood tracker work for any consciousness practice.

How is the AI journal different from ChatGPT?

Our AI is specifically trained on integration therapy frameworks — IFS, somatic awareness, and ACT. It adapts to your stage, your goals, and your history. It notices patterns across weeks of entries.

How do you verify retreats?

Application review, credential verification, reference checks with past participants, safety audit including medical screening and emergency protocols, and ongoing monitoring.

Yes. No contracts. No cancellation fees. No questions asked.
